Prof. Satish Kumar Sinha
Professor (Continuing Education)

  pce@iitism.ac.in        +91-326-223-5207      CV

Prof. (Dr.) Satish Kumar Sinha, FIE Fellow, Institution of Engineers (FIE), worked as mining engineer in different coalfields for a decade as coal production manager as well as environment engineer before joining Indian Institute of Coal Management (IICM) Ranchi as faculty on Mining, Safety, Sustainability and Six Sigma Quality. He has been the Editor of a Mining journal (CMTM- Coal Mining Technology & Management) for two decades and also the Editor of a Management journal (Gondwana) published from Indian Institute of Coal Management Ranchi. He has to his credit over twenty five papers published at various international forums like World Environment Foundation, Greentech, IIT Kharagpur, ISM Dhanbad, ASSOCHAM, MGMI, International Mine Ventilation Congress India and South Africa, University of Wollongong, New South Wales, Australia and Copenhagen Climate Change Summit, International Mine Ventilation Congress,. A visiting faculty in professional institutes like, Birsa Agricultural University, Xavier Institute of Social Services, Administrative Training Institute (SKIPA) Ranchi, Central Coalfields Limited, Bharat Coking Coal Limited, Eastern Coalfields Ltd., Calcutta Energy Supply Corporation etc.
