Introduction

The Student Gymkhana at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad is a recent initiative with a primary objective of giving students a role in the academic and administrative governance of the institute, which would in turn help in inculcating leadership and management qualities in the students.
The Student Gymkhana activities, although subordinate to academic activities, constitute pretty much everything encompassed by the term ‘co-curricular’, entirely managed and organized by the students themselves. The students can choose to be part of it by standing in the annual Gymkhana elections held at the end of March accompanied by proper campaigns, soap boxes, manifestos, booths and the like.
The elected candidates would thereby be privileged to be a part of the Senate with a one-year tenure, that ends on the 31st of March every year.

Election

GENERAL BODY :
All registered members of the institute who pay SG fees are a member of the Student Gymkhana and constitute its General Body.
SG ELECTIONS :
The Student Gymkhana elections are held at the end of March every year, conducted by the Election Commission appointed by the Patron. The week consists of nominations, campaigning, code of conduct, soap boxes etc.

Legislative Wing

SENATE :
The elected candidates form what is called the Students’ Senate (or simply the Senate), which acts as the Central Representative, Legislative and Supervisory body of the students. It is required to function as follows: To serve as the main forum of expression of student opinion To be in continuous touch with the General Body (GB), consult the students and act as a feedback system.
Act as the central policy-making body of the SG Guide and check the functioning of the Executive wing of the SG.

FINANCE CONVENER (FCS) :
The FCS advises the Senate on financial matters. He/she oversees the financial affairs of the SG. He/she: Is elected by the Senate from amongst the Senators. Is responsible to the Senate and through the Senate to the GB for the overall financial affairs of the SG. Is the ex-officio Convener of the Finance Committee of the Senate, who presides over its meetings and is responsible for its activities. Oversees and checks all financial expenditure of the SG. Reports to the Senate from time to time about the status of the finances of the SG. Is an ex-officio member of all Executive councils as well as all Committees of Festival Affairs (Srijan, Concetto and Sports Fest). Does not have any executive responsibilities.

CHAIRPERSON, SENATE (CSS) :
The Chairperson, Senate convenes and presides over the meetings of the Senate. He/she :
(a) Is elected by the Senate from amongst Senators.
(b) Represents the view of the Senate at all forums of the Institute.
(c) Is an ex-officio special invitee to the Academic Senate of the Institute.
(d) Maintains this Constitution and all records of the Senate.
(e) Maintains and executes an Office of the Senate.
(f) Has other responsibilities and powers as mentioned in this Constitution or as assigned to him/her by the Senate from time to time.
(g) Does not have any executive responsibilities unless specifically assigned by the Constitution or the Senate.

LEGISLATIVE WING :
The legislative wing of the IIT (ISM) Gymkhana comprises of the FCS, the CSS, and the Senate itself. It primarily takes most of the administrative decisions, the manner of resolution being purely democratic. It guides and checks the functioning of the Executive Wing, and possesses the power to dispose of the latter if needed.


Executive Wing

EXECUTIVE WING :
The executive wing of the IIT (ISM) Gymkhana comprises of four executive councils - General Affairs, Games & Sports, Media & Culture and Science & Technology - each of which is headed by a Senator (elected by the Senate itself) and guided by a Counsellor (a member of the Institute’s faculty appointed by the Patron). This wing implements all the policies sanctioned by the Senate and the Patron, and oversees the organisation of the several concerned activities held in the college.

THE PRESIDENT, SG (PSG) :
The PSG is the Chief Executive of the SG. He/she:
(a) Is elected by the Senate from amongst Senators.
(b) Supervises and coordinates the general affairs of the GB.
(c) Is the ex-officio Chairperson of the GAC.
(d) Is the ex-officio special invitee to the Academic Senate of the Institute.
(e) Is a member of various bodies of the Institute whenever a representative of the SG is required.
(f) Presides over meetings of the GB, when convened by him/her.
(g) Maintains and executes an Office of the SG (called the PSG’s Office).
(h) Has other responsibilities and powers as mentioned in the Constitution or as assigned to him/her by the Senate from time to time.

GENERAL SECRETARY, MEDIA & CULTURE (SMC) :
The SMC is the Executive of SG overseeing the Media and Cultural activities. He/she:
(a) Is elected by the Senate from amongst Senators.
(b) Supervises and coordinates all Media and Cultural activities of the GB.
(c) Is the ex-officio Chairperson of the MCC.
(d) Is responsible for all executive and financial activities of MCC.
(e) Is responsible to the Senate for the activities of the Media and Cultural Festivals (Srijan and Pratibimb), Inter IIT Cultural Meet or any other competition of the SG.
(f) Has other responsibilities and powers as mentioned in the Constitution.

GENERAL SECRETARY, GAMES & SPORTS (SGS) :
The SGS is the Executive of the SG overseeing Games and Sports activities. He/she:
(a) Is elected by the Senate from amongst Senators.
(b) Supervises and coordinates all Games and Sports activities of the GB.
(c) Is the ex-officio Chairperson of the GSC.
(d) Is responsible for all executive and financial activities of GSC.
(e) Is responsible to the Senate for the activities of the Sports Festival (Parakaram), Annual sports, Inter IIT Sports Meet or any other competition of the SG.
(f) Has other responsibilities and powers as mentioned in the Constitution.

GENERAL SECRETARY, SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY (SST) :
The SST is the Executive of the SG overseeing Science and Technology activities. He/she:
(a) Is elected by the Senate from amongst Senators.
(b) Supervises and coordinates all Science and Technology activities of the GB.
(c) Is the ex-officio Chairperson of the STC.
(d) Is responsible for all executive and financial activities of STC.
(e) Is responsible to the Senate for the activities of the Techno-Management Festival (Concetto) of the SG.
(f) Has other responsibilities and powers as mentioned in the Constitution.

GENERAL AFFAIRS COUNCIL (GAC) :
(a) The GAC assists the PSG in discharging his/her duties.
(b) The Council looks after the affairs of the hostels and other academic and administrative issues pertaining to students. (c) The President, SG is the Chairperson of the GAC and supervises the Council’s activities. (d) The council consists of student members of SUGC and SPGC, President and Secretaries of Hostel Executive Committees of all Hostels, the CSS or his/her nominee and the Finance Convener (ex-officio). MEDIA AND CULTURAL COUNCIL (MCC) :

(a) The MCC organizes and promotes extracurricular activities in the fields of media and culture.
(b) Clubs and hobby groups in the field of media and culture are a part of the MCC.
(c) The SMC is the Chairperson of the MCC and shall supervise the Council’s activities.
(d) The Council consists of Media and Cultural Secretaries of all hostels, the Coordinators of the clubs/hobby groups of the Council, the President or his/her nominee, Finance Convener (ex-officio), the Coordinator (ex-officio) and members of the Finance Committee (ex-officio) associated with the Cultural fest (Srijan) and two senators nominated by the Senate.

GAMES AND SPORTS COUNCIL (GSC) :
(a) The GSC organizes and promotes extracurricular activities in the fields of games and sports.
(b) Clubs and hobby groups in the fields of games and sports are a part of the GSC.
(c) The SGS is the Chairperson of the GSC and supervises the Council’s activities.
(d) The Council consists of Games and Sports Secretaries of all hostels, Captains of the various sports teams of the Institute, the President or his/her nominee, Finance Convener (ex-officio), the Coordinator (ex-officio) and members of the Finance Committee (ex-officio) associated with Sports fest and two senators nominated by the Senate.

SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY COUNCIL (STC) :
(a) The STC organizes and promotes extracurricular activities in the fields of science and technology.
(b) Clubs and hobby groups in the fields of science and technology are a part of the STC.
(c) The SST is the Chairperson of the STC and supervises the Council’s activities.
(d) The Council consists of secretaries of STC of all hostels, the President or his/her nominee, Finance Convener (ex-officio), the Coordinator (ex-officio) and members of the Finance Committee (ex-officio) associated with Techno-Management fest (Concetto) and two senators nominated by the Senate.

Head

CHIEF COUNSELLOR (CC) :
Heading both the legislative and executive wings is the Chief Counsellor, to whom all the Counsellors of their respective councils are subordinate, and who himself is subordinate to the Patron of the SG. The position is assigned to the Dean, Student Welfare (DSW) who hence oversees the administrative activities of the SG as a whole.

PATRON :
The Director of the Institute is the Patron of the SG. The ruling of the Patron in regard to all questions of procedure and on issues not listed in the Constitution is final.

Assisting Committees

FINANCE COMMITTEE (FC) :
(a) The FC manages the finances of the SG and shall control and supervise the expenditures incurred by the Executive wing of the SG.
(b) It reports to the Senate the Finances and Accounts of the SG periodically.
(c) The Finance Convener of the Senate is the Convener of the FC.
(d) The FC also consists of three members who are non-executive members of the Senate elected to this position by the Senate.
(e) Each member of the FC is associated with one council.
(f) The Finance report after each festival is submitted to Senate within one month after its completion and the recommended report is forwarded to Treasurer, SG for approval.

NOMINATIONS COMMITTEE (NC) :
(a) The NC recommends to the Senate the names of persons for any representation of the Senate, Senate ratified positions or department at Institute level counsellors and body, where it is asked to by the constitution or where there is no recommending authority.
(b) Specifically, the NC recommends to the Senate the name(s) of Students’ Senate Representative(s) to the Standing Committees of the Senate.
(c) The NC can call for an explanation, in case its recommended nominee(s) is (are) not found suitable for the assigned task. All its nominations/actions are to be ratified by the Senate.
(d) For each selection pertaining to any representation/position, the NC may constitute a panel to interview the various candidates and select from them the names that shall be recommended to the Senate. The panel may not contain anybody who is contesting for the various posts, even if they are a member of the NC.
(e) The PSG is the ex-officio Convener of the NC and the CSS is an ex-officio member of the NC.
(f) In addition, NC consists of five members with it - Three UG students, One PG student and One PhD student.
