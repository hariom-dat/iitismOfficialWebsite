AIM


The student chapter of SIAM (Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics) was established in the year 2015 at IIT(ISM) DHANBAD. This chapter aims to incorporate the applicable mathematics skills in Engineering students by the means of competitive activities, conferences, talks and interactive sessions.



Vision

chapter envisions to provide global
opportunities to the students to showcase and exchange their innovative ideas and applicable research for industries.



Chapter Benefits

Being a member of SIAM student chapter is a prestigious recognition in both, national and international platforms.
Chapter membership directly allows a student to be the part of world’s largest and vibrant society of Mathematicians & researchers.


Among others, the following are key benefits of chapter membership:

  • Opportunity (with travel support) to represent the chapter in Annual meet of SIAM (usually held every year in USA).
  • SIAM recognizes students for outstanding service and contributions to SIAM Student Chapters.
  • Field trips to industrial laboratories or SIAM section meetings.
  • Student Travel Awards (of $800) for student participants at SIAM conferences with free registration.
  • Subscriptions to SIAM Print and Electronic Publications
  • Discounts on SIAM Books, Conferences, Journals, and more
  • SIAM Activity Group Membership
  • Support of SIAM’s mission and student programs
