Legends In The Making (LITM)

Aim And Vision:

The vision of the club is to provide students an avenue where they can pursue their hobby of dancing and exhibit their non-academic abilities, facilitating development and embodiment of skills which they require in their professional sphere of life..

History:

Initially, LITM was merely a dance crew formed and constituted by few Electrical Engineering students from the class of 2011. Then in the year 2011, it was established officially with the same name after inductions were conducted from all the batches.

Daily Routine:

From its very beginning, LITM has vowed and endeavored to push its perimeter to evolve and explore beyond the horizon. It continues to encroach more styles like classical, contemporary, street, Bollywood, thematic, and even folk for adorning its performance. It has it’s daily practice sessions of 2-3 hours or more throughout the year which usually isn’t the part of culture being followed in other IITs, letting our members gel up together and form stronger bondings among them.

Activities:

  • Kryptoes: It’s the annual dance showcase of LITM cum the annual solo dance competition for freshmen. It usually is the first cultural event of the academic calendar.
  • Inter IIT Cultural Meet: It’s the most coveted event for all cultural societies in the IIT fraternity. LITM has been part of all its edition which is organized every year in the last week of December.
  • Workshop: Esteemed artists of various dance forms are summoned for workshops, educating the members with technicalities and nitty-gritties of his art. At least one or two workshops are conducted each year ensuring the individual growth of each members.
  • Srijan: The dance events of Srijan namely Group, Solo and Duet dance competition are organized by the club in association with team Srijan.

Achivements:

  • Winners of group dance competition in IIT Bhubaneshwar in the year 2014 and 2015.
  • Winners of group dance competition in Srijan’ 17.
  • Runner-up of street battle in the Srijan’ 17.
  • Winners of duet dance competition in the Srijan’ 17.
  • 2nd Runner-up of duet dance competition in the Srijan’ 18.
  • Runner-up of group dance and street battle in Srijan’ 18.
  • 2nd Runner-up of group dance competition in the Srijan’ 19.
  • 2nd Runner-up of group dance competition in 3rd Inter IIT cultural meet 2018.
  • Runner-up of duet dance competition in 3rd inter IIT cultural meet 2018.
