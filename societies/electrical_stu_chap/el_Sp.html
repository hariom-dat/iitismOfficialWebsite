AIM


ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING SOCIETY, IIT(ISM) Dhanbad was established in 2006. The society aims at nurturing the present and shaping the future of students in this globalized world, sustaining good inter-relationship between the esteemed alumni and electrical engineering department.




Vision

Throughout the wholeyear, EES carry out the various welfare programs for the students of the department, and also publicize the opportunities and achievements regarding the students and faculties. The society is made up of a varied collection of individuals who are working together to organize events such as field trips, fresher’s welcome, farewell, guest lectures, and interactive sessions.




Chapter Benefits

Being a member of Electrical engineering society student chapter is a prestigious recognition in both, national and international platforms.


The following are key benefits of chapter membership:

  • You’ll learn more about yourself.
  • You’ll develop soft skills.
  • You’ll learn how to work with a team.
  • You’ll get networking opportunities.
  • You’ll gain practical experience in a safe environment.
  • You’ll be able to use the skills you’ve learned in class.
  • You’ll learn how to engage with diverse groups of people.
  • You’ll gain leadership skills.
  • You’ll get a break from your studies.
  • You’ll expand your resume.
