AIM

Established in 17 July, 2013 Optical Society(OSA) IIT ISM Student chapter is determined to create awareness about the application of light and photonics in next generation young minds and to create better opportunities in this field.

    Our activities can be categorised into three main groups:
  • 1. Educational and professional development activities
  • 2. Youth education and community outreach
  • 3. Social and networking activities.



Vision

As the primary motive of this chapter is to create awareness about the application of light and photonics. OSA provides technical support to chapter by providing self-developed optical laser kit tool box to demonstrate different phenomena of light, these tool kits are beneficial to students of schools and colleges. OSA IIT(ISM) student chapter organize Travelling lecture program where renowned lecturer from prestigious universities of the world share their experience and knowledge with students and motivate them to explore new possibilities in the field of optics and photonics. Each year our student chapters organize national workshop on Recent Trend in Photonics Technology for graduate and undergraduate students where students gets a brief introduction on various hardware and Softwares courses related to optics and photonics. Besides this, various Technical Development Program are organized by PG students and research scholars where they share their ideas and experience of their research topic. This greatly helps the students to grasp the new research area where they can use their potential to explore more in related fields.
Moreover, OSA provide travel grant to the OSA student chapter member to attend different conferences
like FIO, IONS, Summer school like Siegmann school of light etc in Abroad and



Chapter Benefits

The Chapter is useful for the students as they can gain out of the opportunities/ conferences provided to them through their 4 years of College


opportunities/ conferences provided to students:

  • SPIE IIT(ISM) student chapter organize International Visiting lecture program where experts from prestigious universities of the world share their experience and knowledge with students and motivate them to explore new possibilities in the field of optics and photonics.

SPIE student chapter also provide travel grant to the chapter officer member to attend different conferences like SPIE Photonics West, SPIE Optics+Photonics etc.

SPIE chapter Visiting lecture program 2018:

Date:

26th and 27thOctober 2018

Speaker:

Prof. Lorenzo Pavesi (University of Trento)

The IIT (ISM) Dhanbad SPIE chapter organized a visiting lecture programon 26th and 27thOctober 2018. In this program Prof. Lorenzo Pavesi from University of Trento, Italy was invited to delivera lecture on Photonics. We have also organised faculty interaction session in this program.
Lorenzo Pavesi is Professor of Experimental Physics at the Department of Physics of the University of Trento (Italy). He received his PhD in Physics in 1990 at the Ecole Polytechnique Federale of Lausanne (Switzerland). He leads the Nanoscience Laboratory (25 people), teaches several classes at the Department of Physics of the University of Trento. He is interested in active photonics devices which can be integrated in silicon by using optical nonlinearities and modified material properties. His interests encompass also optical sensors or biosensors and solar cells.He is an author or co-author of more than 500 papers, author of several reviews, editor of more than 15 books, author of 2 books and holds 7 patents.In 2010 and 2011 he was elected distinguished speaker of the IEEE- Photonics society. He is fellow of the IEEE and senior member of SPIE.
