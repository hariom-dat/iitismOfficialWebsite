MANTHAN

About

We sing, we play, we jam, we perform. We practice different songs and strive to achieve the best version of the song according to our potential. We are the music club of IIT (ISM) which is special in its own way. We cover all the musical events of our college now let it be a rock metal song or an Indian classical song.

Aim

To give a platform and launching pad for the musical talents and to develop a sense of music among all the members.

Story Behind Formation

You might have seen a road between Diamond hostel and Opal hostel ending behind the former. It leads to a small room with a capacity of holding one drumkit only. There, in 2006, started a band named ‘Manthan’, with A few students of this college as its members but the band collapsed with the graduation of its members. After the absence of any distinguished music club or band in ISM for two years, finally in 2012, with the help of music enthusiast friends and seniors, Tony Daheria and Abhay Singh formed a music society named ‘Manthan’. It definitely took some years of hard work by its members before it took its present form as the official music club of IIT ISM Dhanbad with a strength of about 60 people, including its annual events and numerous feathers in its cap from the various competitions.

Achievements

Inter-IIT Cultural Meet Records:

  • 2nd position in solo singing competition for two consecutive years
  • 5th and 9th position in band competition among 23 bands

    • Other notable performances:
  • A musical tribute to URI attack victims through the event ‘ALIVE'16’.
  • Outside the college at a CRPF camp.

Club Activities

We have music theory classes thrice a week by experienced seniors of the club and we practice the technical things for the rest of the week.
Manthan organizes their two annual events named ‘ALIVE’ – the event for the freshers of the college and ‘The Last Stage’ – wherein the final year of music club performs for one last time in the college. We also organize an acoustic session in the month of January. Apart from that, the club members perform all year round at the official as well as unofficial events as required.
