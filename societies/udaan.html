UDAAN

About

Udaan was founded in 2015.

Conception & Vision:

Udaan was established to fill in the void of a career information portal in ISM. Our vision is to aid students make well-informed career decisions. You took admission in an engineering institute, it doesn’t mean you need to necessarily be an engineer forever. We act as a bridge of information for students to confidently pursue alternate career paths. To this end, we organise sessions, interview alumni and share student experiences. Since our inception, the institute has witnessed a spate of students embracing entrepreneurship, finance, foreign internships, higher studies etc.as opposed to the dogma of campus placements.

Journey So Far:

What began just as a humble group of people who wished to break out from the widely-held belief of pursuing just the ordinary, Udaan has grown out as an established society. Our team works tirelessly round the year to conduct interviews, sessions and workshops for the student fraternity.

Club Activities:

We regularly conduct interviews with IIT(ISM) alumni to gain insights into the latest industry trends, what skills are in demand currently, how difficult is it to break into the industry etc. Our sessions aim to invite junior students for interaction with accomplished seniors. The topics for such sessions include foreign internships, higher studies, GATE/GRE, finance, entrepreneurship. We also distribute study materials for preparation, if required. In addition to this, the workshops aim at inculcating soft skills and equip students with skills such as graphic designing, networking using LinkedIn, building industry relations etc.

Flagship Event - ‘Rejuvenating Ism’

Rejuvenating ISM is a celebration of our successful alumni. The day-long celebrations include seminars, sessions and workshops. Although the previous iterations of the event has been kept low-profile, we are currently in process of inviting over organisations and individuals from outside the campus to increase our reach.
